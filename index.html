The Mobster Manifesto
Our rules for mob programming

  • Communication other than voice should be in the slack group.
  • Always be on time. Things can happen, but you should meet up in discord at the time of meeting. If we are meeting at 08:30 you should be there at 08:30, not at 08:31.
  • Be open and honest with each other. We should be able to trust each other.
  • Try not to be easily offended, but if you are, don't be afraid to speak up and help us all create a work environment we want to be a part of.
  • Be curious, ask questions when you are unsure or wondering about something.
  • Let people speak out - seek to understand before you seek to be understood
  • In mobs the driver should be typing, and not taking initiative. However that doesn't mean that person is brain dead either, and should speak up if they do not understand something.
  • It is everybody's responsibility to make sure everyone is learning - Be respectful & be friendly - we learn more if we're having fun as well.
